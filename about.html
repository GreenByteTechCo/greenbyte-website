About GreenByte

We build technology
that actually matters.

A global tech company born in northern Nigeria, building AI, blockchain, and software for communities that deserve world-class solutions.

Our Story

GreenByte Tech Co started with a problem and a belief. The problem: Africa's most pressing challenges were going unsolved — not because solutions didn't exist, but because the people closest to those problems weren't building them.

The belief: that a computer science student from Gezawa, Kano State could build products that compete at the highest global level — products that are not just good for Africa, but world-class by any standard.

The name says it all. Green represents Africa, agriculture, growth, and the land that raised us. Byte represents technology, computation, and the digital future we are building. Together, GreenByte is the bridge — between Africa's roots and the world's future.

Incorporated in April 2026 under the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria through SMEDAN, GreenByte Tech Co is a legally registered technology company operating across AI, blockchain, mobile software, and digital education — with a product portfolio targeting both African markets and the global stage.

We are not a startup waiting to be discovered. We are builders — shipping products, applying for grants, winning hackathons, and growing a company from the ground up, one commit at a time.
